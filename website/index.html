Derived intelligence, not raw metrics

Health platforms expose sleep and HRV. Cortex Bio exposes readiness, chronotype, peak windows, and forecasts.

Readiness

Explainable cognitive readiness from sleep, HRV, activity, and chronotype.

GET /v1/readiness/today

Chronotype

Morning lark to night owl — data-driven from your sleep timing.

GET /v1/chronotype

Performance windows

Peak focus, secondary peak, and crash windows for scheduling deep work.

GET /v1/windows/today

24h forecast

Hourly performance curve with best deep-work and recovery windows.

GET /v1/forecast

Predictions

ML forecasts for attention, deep work, and output — baseline-validated.

GET /v1/predictions/tomorrow
